Books and Brews is place where everyone can socialize and relax while enjoying top quality drinks and food - at an affordable price - and surrounded by a library of used books

Our goal is to create a welcoming environment to everyone regardless of age, race, gender, or social status. This experience is not limited to individuals and will encompass families, friends, and communities. Being a book store it's only fitting to quote a book - In Howard Shultz’s book, pour your heart into it, he says, “We offer an affordable luxury. In our stores you may see a policeman or utility worker standing in line in front of a wealthy surgeon. The blue-collar man may not be able to afford the Mercedes the surgeon just drove up in, but he can order the same $2.00 cappuccino. They’re both giving themselves a reward and enjoying something world class.” (p. 119)

Everyone deserves the best. That is what Books and Brews will offer - the best product and experience possible. We'll serve everyone
